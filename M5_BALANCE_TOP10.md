4. **Respawn invulnerability window (short)** to reduce spawn-camping loops.
5. **Zone biomass floor safeguards** to avoid hard starvation lock states.
6. **Evolution draft rarity weighting** by level bracket for smoother power curve.
7. **Early-level HP scaling trim** to reduce snowball from first draft spikes.
8. **Movement normalization across evolution stacks** to avoid runaway speed builds.
9. **Apex tie-break visibility** in UI (level > xp > apexScore) for clarity.
10. **Anti-ghost ownership timeout tuning** so disconnected controllers lose zones predictably.
